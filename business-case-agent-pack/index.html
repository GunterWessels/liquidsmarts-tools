How It Works

Three steps. No integration.

Works in any LLM you already use — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, or Copilot. Free tier accounts are fine.

1

Open 00_your_context.md. Fill in Layer A once — your company, product, and top proof points. Fill in Layer B for each deal: five fields, five minutes.

2

Open 01_master_agent.md. Copy the entire file. Start a new conversation in your LLM of choice. Paste, insert your filled context where indicated, and send.

3
Review Agent 6 first

Before you present or share anything, read what Agent 6 flagged. Fix every Illustrative-confidence row before the business case goes to committee. Then use Agents 4 and 5 as your deliverables.
